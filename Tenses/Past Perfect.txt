Positive: She had finished her homework before dinner. (Akşam yemeğinden önce ev ödevini bitirmişti.)
Negative: She had not finished her homework before dinner. (Akşam yemeğinden önce ev ödevini bitirememişti)
Question: Had she finished her homework before dinner?

# Usage
An action that happened before another past action
 - When I arrived, they had already left.
 - She had studied English before she moved to Canada.
Cause-effect in the past.
 - He was tired because he had worked all day.
Reported speech (backshifting)
 - He said he had seen the movie.

# SIGNAL WORDS
 - Before / After
 - Already / Just
 - by the time I'll build it.
