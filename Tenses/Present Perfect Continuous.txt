Subject plus have/has plus been / verb ing
 - I/You/We/They have been working.
 - He/She/It has been working.

Actions that started in the past and continue until now (Geçmişte başlayan ve bugüne kadar devam eden eylemler)
 - I have been learning English for 2 years
 - She has been living in istanbul since 2020.
Actions that were happening recently (and may still be happening)
 - He has been studying all morning (= maybe still studiying or just finished)
 - They have been playing football, that's why they are tired

# EXAMPLES
 - I have been waiting for the bus for 20 minutes.
 - She has been talking on the phone since 8 pm.
 - They have been building a new school in our town.
 etc.
