Subject + have/has + past participle

- I/You/We/They have eaten
- He/She/It has eaten.

We use Present Perfect for:
 - I have visited Paris. (= at some point in my life)
 - She has tried sushi
Recent actions (with result now)
 - He has broken his leg. (= still broken)
 - They have finished their homework.
Unfinished time periods
 - I have studied three hours today.
 - We have lived here for five years.

# EXMAPLEs
 - I have seen that movie before
 - She has lost her keys.
 - We have just arrived home.

# SIGNAL WORDS
 - Already/Just/Never/Ever/Yet
 - For / Since
 - Today / This week / This year
