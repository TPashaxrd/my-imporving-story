# The reulst is always correct.

Examples:
1. If you heat water to 100 celcius, it boils.
2. if it rains, the ground gets wet
3. If you touch fire, you get burned.
4. If you mix red and blue, you get purple

Little Rules:
 - Both clauses are in Present Simple.
 - If clause can be at the beginning or end:
   - If you study hard, you pass the exam.
   - You pass the exam if you study hard.
