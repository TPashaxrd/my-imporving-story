Positive: You should eat more coca cola.
Negative: You should not (shouldn't) eat junk food
Question: Should I call him?

# Using
Tavsiye
 - You should sleep earlier. (Daha erken uyumalısın.)
 - He should see a dactor. (Doktor'a gitmeli.)
Beklenti / Tahmin
 - The train should arrive at seven (7). (Tren 7'de gelmeli.)
 - It should be easy. (Kolay olmalı)
Kibarca öneri / fikir sorma
 - Should I open the window? (Camı açayım mı?)
 - What should we do now? (Şimdi ne yapmalıyız?)

Example Sentences:
 - We shouldn't waste time. (Zaman Kaybetmemeliyiz.)
 - Should I help you with your homework?

Mini Dialogue
 - A: I feel sick.
 - B: You should see a doctor.
 - A: Should I take medicine?
 - B: Yes, you should.
