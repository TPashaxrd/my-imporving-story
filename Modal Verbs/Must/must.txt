Positive: You must study hard.
Negative: You must not (mustn't) smoke here.
Question: Must I go now?

Example:
 - You must wear a helmet. (Kask takmalısın./Zorunlu)
 - Studens must study for the exam. (Öğrenciler sınav için çalışmalıdır)

Rules: 
# NEGATIVE
Musn't = > Yasak anlamına gelir
Example: You mustn't park here. (Buraya park etmemelisin / yasak.)
# QUESTION
Example: Must we be quiet? - Must I finish this today?

How to speak(Mast):
 - you must go - > yu mast go
 - He must study -> hi mast stadi
 - They must be tired - < dey mast bi tayrıd
 - Generally: Mast - > Must
